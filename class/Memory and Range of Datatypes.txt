byte: 8 bits, ranges from -128 to 127.
short: 16 bits, ranges from -32,768 to 32,767.
int: 32 bits, ranges from -2^31 to 2^31 - 1.
long: 64 bits, ranges from -2^63 to 2^63 - 1.
float: 32 bits,  7 decimal places of precision.
double: 64 bits, 15 decimal places of precision.
char:16 bits,represents a Unicode character.
boolean:true or false.
